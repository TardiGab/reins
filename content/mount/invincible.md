## Source

Dropped by [The Lich King](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=36597/the-lich-king) in Icecrown Citadel on Heroic 25 difficulty.

### Other known sources

- Can be looted from the [Cache of Timewraped Treasures](https://www.wowhead.com/item=208094/cache-of-timewarped-treasures) during WoTLK Timewalking events.
- Can be obtained from an [Unclaimed Black Market Container](https://www.wowhead.com/item=169430/unclaimed-black-market-container) on the Black Market Auction House.
