#desc
## Source

Dropped by [Nightbane](https://www.wowhead.com/ptr-2/npc=114895/nightbane), a secret boss in the Return to Karazhan dungeon on Mythic difficulty. This mount has a quite high drop rate (20% if solo). This scales and increases with the number of players in the group, up to 100% drop rate for a full group of 5 players. 

::guides-hint
Note: the drop rate is shared among all players in the group, so if one player gets the mount, it won't drop for the others.
::

## Introduced in:

Patch 7.1.0 "Return to Karazhan"

#guide
## How to get the Smoldering Ember Wyrm?
[Nightbane](https://www.wowhead.com/ptr-2/npc=114895/nightbane) is the secret (and final) boss in the Return to Karazhan dungeon on Mythic difficulty. 

Upon entering the dungeon, you will see a message in the chat log that says "The strange chill of a dark presence winds through the air." If the message doesn't appear, it is likely that you're on the wrong difficulty. 

::guides-image

::

Once you see the message, you can proceed through the dungeon as normal. While progressing, you will encounter 5 crystals called [Soul Fragment](https://www.wowhead.com/ptr-2/npc=115013/soul-fragment) that you need to interact with. These crystals gives you a buff called [Medivh's Echo](https://www.wowhead.com/ptr-2/spell=229074/medivhs-echo) that stacks. At 5 stacks of the buff, it becomes [Medivh's Presence](https://www.wowhead.com/ptr-2/spell=229077/medivhs-presence). [**Do not die during this run**]{style="color: red;"}, as you will lose all your stacks and have to start over.

::guides-hint
Note: If you're in a group, all group members receive stacks of Medivh's Echo. If a group member dies, the other members keep their stacks, meaning you can still have the 5 stacks needed to face Nightbane.
::

1. The first crystal is located in the first boss room: The Opera. Kill the boss and go to the spectator area (in the opposite stage) to find the crystal in the middle of the room. Click on it to get the first stack of Medivh's Echo.
::
